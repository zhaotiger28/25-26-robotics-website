About Us

Who We Are

Hornet Robotics is Greenhill School's premier robotics team. We compete in FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) competitions, where we design, build, and program robots to complete complex tasks.

What We Do

Four pillars that drive our success

Engineering

We design and build competition-ready robots using CAD software, 3D printing, and precision manufacturing techniques. Every component is carefully planned and tested.

Competition

We compete at regional and state FTC events throughout Texas, testing our skills against teams from across the region and constantly pushing our limits.

Teamwork

With 24 members across all grades, collaboration and mentorship are at our core. Upperclassmen guide newer members, creating a supportive learning environment.

Outreach

We mentor younger students and share our passion for STEM with the broader community through workshops, school visits, and hands-on demonstrations.
